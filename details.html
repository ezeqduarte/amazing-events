Book fair.

A book fair is a cultural event that constitutes a meeting point between supply (providers of publishing products) and demand (readers-buyers). The objective of this work is to analyze the different functions of a book fair based on the experience accumulated during the planning and execution of the International Book Fair of the University of Carabobo, with a qualitative and descriptive method. Book fairs are important communication tools that represent spaces for promoting reading, valuing literature and knowledge. In addition, a book fair becomes a space for access to printed and digital books, for the training of readers and promoters of reading as the main way to achieve personal and community development.
